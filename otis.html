Otis Nepal

Our association with Otis, the worldwide leader in elevators, escalators and moving walkways, has been over a decade now. It is a highly regarded brand in the world and has earned a good reputation here in Nepal as well. We have a dedicated team of highly skilled professionals in this division, who are able to understand the need of the clients and offer solutions both in terms of sales and installation. Till now, around 300 elevators have been already sold in Nepal. Our list of clients includes hotels, hospitals, business complexes, shopping malls, housing and apartments among others.

For further information visit Otis.
