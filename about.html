About Us

Continental Trading Enterprises has been under operation for over two decades delivering the best in class brands and unbridled service to Nepal. We represent famous brands like Komatsu construction and mining equipments, Kia passenger vehicles, Otis elevators and drilling equipments like Furukawa. We also have our stake in manufacturing concerns, financial institutions and housing development. Over the course of our long history and vast experience, we have been able to create our name itself as a valuable brand in Nepal. Now developed as one of the prominent business conglomerate, we have our keen eyes on expansion for the better of the company as well as the nation. We have been able to have remarkable market size in all our represented brands. Our strength, as we see, are the highly focused customer service that we provide, our young and dynamic group of staff and the trust that has been bestowed upon us by our customers, banks and stakeholders.
